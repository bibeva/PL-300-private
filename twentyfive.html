Question 250

You are using a line chart in Power BI Desktop to visualize weekly user activity on your platform. To better understand the data's variability and central tendency, which features from the Analytics pane should you consider adding to your chart? (Select all that apply)

  • A. Constant line
  • B. Median line
  • C. Percentile line
  • D. Standard deviation bands
  • A. Constant line
  • B. Median line
  • C. Percentile line
  • D. Standard deviation bands

Explanation: To understand variability and central tendency in a line chart, you can use the Median line to see the middle value, Percentile lines to assess the distribution, and Standard deviation bands to visualize how data deviates from the mean. A Constant line is fixed at a specific value and does not provide insights into the data’s variability or central tendency.

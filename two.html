Question 20

Your organization uses Power BI to track marketing campaign performance.
You have a workspace dedicated to these reports. You need to grant the Marketing team access to the reports while ensuring that only authorized personnel can view and interact with the sensitive campaign data.
What is the most effective way to achieve this?

  • A. Share each individual report with every member of the Marketing team.
  • B. Grant the Marketing team "Admin" access to the workspace.
  • C. Publish the reports as an app and distribute it to the Marketing team's Azure Active Directory group.
  • D. Add all Marketing team members as individual members of the workspace.
  • A. Share each individual report with every member of the Marketing team.
  • B. Grant the Marketing team "Admin" access to the workspace.
  • C. Publish the reports as an app and distribute it to the Marketing team's Azure Active Directory group.
  • D. Add all Marketing team members as individual members of the workspace.

Explanation: Publishing the reports as a Power BI app and assigning access to the team’s Azure AD group ensures that only authorized users can view and interact with the reports. This method is more secure and scalable than sharing individual reports or adding users directly to the workspace.

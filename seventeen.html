Question 170

Which role allows a user to view reports and dashboards in a Power BI workspace but does not permit downloading the reports?

  • A. Viewer
  • B. Contributor
  • C. Member
  • D. Admin
  • A. Viewer
  • B. Contributor
  • C. Member
  • D. Admin

Explanation: In Power BI, the Viewer role allows users to access reports and dashboards within a workspace for viewing purposes only. They can interact with the content (like using slicers or filters) but cannot edit, publish, or download the reports. Other roles like Contributor, Member, and Admin provide increasing levels of edit and management permissions.

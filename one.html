Question 10

To analyze and compare sales performance across different regions, which visualization would you use?

  • A. Line Chart
  • B. Pie Chart
  • C. Bar Chart
  • D. Scatter Plot
  • A. Line Chart
  • B. Pie Chart
  • C. Bar Chart
  • D. Scatter Plot

Explanation: A Bar Chart is ideal for comparing values across different categories, such as sales performance across regions. It clearly shows differences in magnitude between regions, making it easier to analyze and compare. Line charts are better for trends over time, pie charts for proportions, and scatter plots for relationships between two numeric variables.

Question 70

What type of visualization would best represent monthly sales trends to highlight seasonal patterns?

  • A. Pie Chart
  • B. Line Chart
  • C. Heat Map
  • D. Column Chart
  • A. Pie Chart
  • B. Line Chart
  • C. Heat Map
  • D. Column Chart

Explanation: A line chart is ideal for displaying trends over time because it connects data points sequentially, making it easy to observe fluctuations and seasonal patterns in monthly sales. Pie charts and column charts show proportions or discrete comparisons, while heat maps are better for highlighting intensity across categories rather than temporal trends.

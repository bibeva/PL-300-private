Question 140

You utilize the following Power Query M formula to produce a sequence of dates: = List.Dates(#date(2021,12,15), 180, #duration(1,0,0,0))
What does this date sequence represent?

  • A. A sequence of hours starting from December 15, 2021, for the next 180 hours
  • B. A series of days beginning on December 15, 2021, and continuing for 180 days thereafter
  • C. A collection of weeks commencing from December 15, 2021, spanning the next 26 weeks
  • A. A sequence of hours starting from December 15, 2021, for the next 180 hours
  • B. A series of days beginning on December 15, 2021, and continuing for 180 days thereafter
  • C. A collection of weeks commencing from December 15, 2021, spanning the next 26 weeks

Explanation: List.Dates creates a list starting from the given date, repeats it for the specified count, and increments by the provided duration. Here, the duration is one day, so it generates 180 consecutive daily dates starting from December 15, 2021.
